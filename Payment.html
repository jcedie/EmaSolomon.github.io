Payment Terms


1. Initial Deposit

-A non-refundable deposit of 20% of the total event cost is required upon signing the contract to secure your event date. This ensures that we can begin planning and reserving vendors. -The percentage may vary based on the eventâ€™s size and complexity.

2. Payment Installments

-The remaining balance will be split into two or three installments:
-First Installment (30%):Typically due 3-6 months before the event. This installment helps cover ongoing planning and vendor deposits.
-Second Installment (30%): Due 1 month before the event. This installment ensures that all final preparations are made, including vendor payments.

3. Final Payment

-The remaining balance (20%) is due one week before the event date, or upon approval of all final event details.
-No services will be rendered until the final payment is completed.

4. Additional Costs

- Last-minute changes or additions (e.g., additional decor, extra services) will be billed separately and must be settled before the event date.
- Travel or accommodation fees for destination events will be included in the final invoice.

5. Accepted Payment Method

-Bank Transfer
-Credit/Debit Card(Visa, MasterCard, etc.)
-PayPal
-Cash Payments(for local clients)
Note: Processing fees for card payments may apply.

6. Refund Policy

- In the case of event postponement. deposits can be applied to the rescheduled date (subject to availability) within a 6-month period.
- No refunds are available for services already rendered or payments already made.


7. Amendments

-Any amendments to this agreement must be made in writing and signed by both parties.

8. Flexible Payment Plan

-We offer custom payment plans for clients with specific financial needs. Let us know your budget, and we will create a flexible plan that works for you.

9. Acceptance

-By signing the contract and making the initial deposit, the client acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agreed to the terms and conditions set forth in this agreement.
